Process

  • 1st choice

    "We offer you the opportunity to create your own cable. It is up to you to mark what you need."

    (Guitar, microphone, sound system, etc.)

  • 2nd choice

    "It is up to you to decide on the desired cable length.
    If none of the standard lengths suits you, send us an email with a measure that would suit you."

  • 3rd choice

    "Each cable we make is protected with 48 or 64 threads of quality thread, depending on the purpose of the cable. That's why we give you the opportunity to choose whether you want one or two colors. In case of choosing two colors, you get the possibility to choose a knitting pattern."

  • 4th choice

    "In the very end, you can choose the color of the protection of the connector itself. The protection passes over the connector and one small part of the cable and thus prevents possible damage to the connection between the cable and the connector."
